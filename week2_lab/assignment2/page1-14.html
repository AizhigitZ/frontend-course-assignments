How to Make Pancakes

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until smooth.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladle of batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until golden brown on both sides and serve with syrup.
